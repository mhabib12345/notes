## Eid Mubarak 1446H
As the crescent moon is sighted and the holy month of Ramadan concludes,
we celebrate Eid al-Fitr 1446 H with joy and gratitude.

May this Eid bring you and your family:
- Peace like the serenity of a mosque
- Strength like the trunk of a palm
- Sweetness like dates from its branches
- Growth like its ever-reaching fronds

Eid Mubarak from our family to yours!
